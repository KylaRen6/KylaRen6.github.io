tactile



a poem


Why is it
That our bodies hold each other so well?
Hands fit into hands

Perfectly,

Comfortably
We can hug each other and it is easy

Our bones are made from

Hard,

Sharp


Calcium



(So are some stars)

But the bodies around them are soft Good for holding, good for comfort

Contact is sustenance For some a boon worth years of searching Others find it easily

Touch is simple, natural and nonthreatening,

Or a rare, caught breath that floats over eggshells

Why is it That it hurts so much to be lonely? Hands fit into hands

Tentatively,

Hopefully

We were crafted by the gods to comfort one another
